- Theme: Light only, minimalist, professional, high-end
- Background: Clean White (#ffffff) and Soft Gray (#f9fafb) 
- Primary Accent: Vibrant Orange (#ec6803) for central CTA buttons and active states
- Text Primary: Dark Gray (#111827) for headings
- Text Secondary: Medium Gray (#4b5563) for body text
- Typography: Outfit font (modern geometric sans-serif)
- Borders: Subtle and thin, gray-100
- Corners: Subtly rounded (8px to 12px)

- Aesthetic: Spacious, crisp, using minimalist line icons. Monochrome accents with Orange highlights.
